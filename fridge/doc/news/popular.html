Awk.info Gaining Popularity

Nov 28, 2009

Four months ago, typing "awk" into Google resulted in pages of output where the first mention of awk.info did not appear till half-way down page five. Today, the same query finds "awk.info" on page two (in position 18). So this site is moving up the page rankings.
