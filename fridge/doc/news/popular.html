Awk.info Gaining Popularity

Four months ago, typing "awk" into Google resulted in pages of output where the first mention of awk.info did not appear till half-way down page five.

Today, the same query finds "awk.info" on page two (in position 18). So this site is moving up the page rankings.

In other news, http://www.websiteoutlook.com/www.awk.info reports that the site is now worth $1423.5 USD and it promises a daily ad revenue stream from awk.info of $1.95. To put that in perspective, rottentomatoes.com is worth $3,300,000 with a daily revenue stream of $4523.19.
